Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

GR Link ("the App") is an iOS companion application for Ricoh GR series cameras. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App handles and how it is used.

Data Collection

GR Link does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

The App operates entirely on your device. All communication occurs directly between your iPhone and your Ricoh GR camera over local WiFi or Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) connections.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Camera connection settings and preferences
  • Saved recipes and calibration data
  • Photos downloaded from your camera
  • App usage preferences (e.g., theme, language)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Network Communication

GR Link communicates only with your Ricoh GR camera over:

  • WiFi — Direct connection to the camera's built-in WiFi for LiveView, photo transfer, and camera control
  • Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) — For remote shutter release and camera discovery

No data is sent to the internet, cloud services, or analytics platforms.

Third-Party Services

GR Link does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

GR Link does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store listing or by visiting www.grlink.org.
